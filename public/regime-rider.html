The Concept: Market Regimes

Markets don't behave the same way all the time. They cycle through regimes — distinct states with different statistical properties. A strategy that crushes it in a trending market will bleed in a choppy one. The key skill: identify what regime you're in, then apply the appropriate strategy. This is regime detection.

📈 Trending

Strong directional move. Low-moderate vol. SMA spread is wide and growing. Follow the trend — momentum works here.

↔ Choppy

No clear direction. Low vol. Price oscillates around a mean. Fade extremes — mean reversion works here.

⚡ Volatile

High, unpredictable vol. Large random swings. No strategy edge. Stay flat — protect capital.

How to Play

1

Every 15 seconds a new classification round begins. You see the price chart, a volatility meter, and a trend-strength meter.

2

Read the two indicators and classify the regime (Trending / Choppy / Volatile) then pick a strategy (Follow Trend / Fade Extremes / Stay Flat).

3

Scoring: +3 pts for correct regime AND correct strategy. +1 pt for correct regime only. −1 pt for wrong regime.

4

After each round, you see the correct answer and an explanation. Game lasts 8 rounds (~2 minutes).

The Two Indicators to Read

Volatility Meter shows 20-bar realised vol (annualised). Low = below 15%, Medium = 15–35%, High = above 35%. Trend Strength shows how wide the SMA-10/SMA-30 spread is as a % of price. Strong (>0.8%) means clear trend. Weak (<0.3%) means flat or oscillating. Together they identify the regime: low vol + strong trend = Trending; low vol + weak trend = Choppy; high vol = Volatile (regardless of trend).
